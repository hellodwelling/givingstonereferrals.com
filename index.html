About Us

GivingStone Referrals connects buyers and sellers with trusted real estate agents across the country and donates half of our earnings at closing to any charity of your choice. Jeremy is a licensed real estate agent and has traveled the US making connections with the top agents across the country. He founded GivingStone Referrals to give back to our communities through doing something he loves. Now, homeowners can match with the best agent for them, buy or sell real estate, and make a difference doing it.
